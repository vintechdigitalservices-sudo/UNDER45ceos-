About UNDER45CEOs

UNDER45CEOs is a high-impact leadership and enterprise platform designed to connect forward-thinking brands with Africa's next generation of entrepreneurs, executives, innovators, and decision-makers aged 18-45.

Now in its 6th edition, the platform continues to grow in reach, credibility, and national and continental relevance, bringing together thousands of physical attendees and projecting over 8.5 million media impressions across digital and traditional platforms.
